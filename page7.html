How to Arrive?

By the air:
You can search here flights to Kalamata airport or to Athens airport "Eleftherios Venizelos. The distance from Kalamata International Airport to Monemvasia are rund about 180km.

By the car:
If you drive from Athens (300km), you will take the direction shows to Korinthos, and then to Tripoli, to Sparti and finally to Monemvasia.

When you reach Monemvasia, go all straight ahead. At less than 3 minutes you will get into the center of the new town of Monemvasia, before you pass the bridge leading to the Rock and Castle of Monemvasia, you have to turn left to the pharmacy and follow the road through the coast. Our hotel is right in front of the beach, on your left hand.

Activities

Walking through the alleys of the Castle takes you on a journey to the past. Allow yourself to be captivated by the narrow streets that crisscross Monemvasia, transporting you to another era. The main alley is lined with charming shops, cafes, and restaurants, creating an atmosphere where time appears to have stood still in the Middle Ages.

Take the time to explore the following attractions:

Monemvasia's Archaeological Collection: Located in an old mosque in the main square of the lower town within the castle.

Cathedral of Monemvasia - Elkomenos Christos: A visit to this historical cathedral offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage.

Church of Panagha Chrysafitissa: Explore the lovely, small, white church with its unique charm.

Lighthouse of Monemvasia: Situated behind Panagha Chrysafitissa’s church, the lighthouse offers a unique perspective of the surroundings.

Upper City Exploration: Walk to the top of the rock, exploring the Upper City, and savor the breathtaking panoramic views.

Church of Aghias Sophia: Visit the famous church on the Upper Town, known for its historical significance.

Watermill at Talanta Village: A short drive from Monemvasia, this watermill in Talanta village is worth exploring.

Running and Cycling: Explore the streets of Monemvasia's new town through running and cycling.

Hiking to Kavos Maleas Lighthouse: Embark on a hiking adventure to the Lighthouse at Kavos Maleas, located in the southern part of the Peloponnese.

Archaeological Museum of Neapolis: A 30-minute drive from Monemvasia, this museum in Neapolis showcases the region's archaeological treasures.

Nautical Museum of Neapolis: Another attraction in Neapolis, this museum provides insights into the maritime history of the area.

Larnaka Gorge at Molaoi: Explore the stunning Larnaka Gorge, just a 30-minute drive from Monemvasia.

Cave of Kastania: A one-hour drive will take you to the spectacular Cave of Kastania.

Caves of Diros: A unique experience awaits at the Caves of Diros, a 1.5-hour drive from Monemvasia.

Swimming: Enjoy the crystal-clear waters at various beaches, including Simo’s beach to Elafonisos island, Plytra, Marathias, Archaggelos, Pori, Ampelakia, Xifias, and more.

Visit Kiparissi Village: Immerse yourself in the charm of Kiparissi village.

Fjord of Gerakas: Explore the scenic fjord of Gerakas, offering picturesque views.

Each of these activities adds to the enchanting experience of Monemvasia, providing a diverse range of historical, cultural, and natural wonders to discover.
